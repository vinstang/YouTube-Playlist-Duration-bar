YouTube Playlist Duration Bar

Know exactly how long your YouTube playlists are — and how far you've come.

YouTube doesn't tell you how long a playlist is, or how much of it you've watched. This extension fixes that instantly.


WHILE WATCHING A PLAYLIST VIDEO
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
• Total playlist duration
• Watched time so far
• Progress percentage (shown as a number and a visual bar)
• Remaining time until the playlist ends

Everything updates live as you watch — no page reloads needed.


WHILE BROWSING A PLAYLIST PAGE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
• Total duration of all videos in the playlist
• Video count displayed at a glance


WORKS EVERYWHERE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
✔ Public playlists, private playlists, Watch Later, Liked Videos
✔ Dark mode and light mode — matches YouTube's theme automatically
✔ Updates in real time when videos are added, removed, or reordered
✔ No sign-in required, no permissions beyond YouTube


PERFECT FOR
━━━━━━━━━━━
📚 Students planning study sessions with lecture playlists
🎵 Music listeners tracking progress through long mixes
🎬 Binge-watchers of documentary series or podcast playlists
🏋️ Workout playlists where timing actually matters
📋 Playlist curators who want to know the total runtime


PRIVACY
━━━━━━━
This extension collects no data whatsoever. It reads only the video duration elements already visible on your screen and does all processing locally in your browser.

Note: YouTube's UI loads approximately 200 videos at a time. Scroll down to load more videos for an accurate count on very large playlists.
