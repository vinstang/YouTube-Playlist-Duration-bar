Know exactly how long your YouTube playlists are — and how far you've come.

YouTube doesn't tell you how long a playlist is, or how much of it you've already watched. This extension fixes that instantly, with a clean progress bar and live duration stats injected directly into the YouTube UI.

While watching a playlist video

  • Total playlist duration
  • Watched time so far
  • Progress percentage — shown as a number and a visual bar
  • Remaining time until the playlist ends

Everything updates live as you watch. No page reloads, no button clicks.

While browsing a playlist page

  • Total duration of all videos in the playlist
  • Video count at a glance

Works everywhere on YouTube

  • Public playlists, private playlists, Watch Later, Liked Videos
  • Dark mode and light mode — matches YouTube's current theme automatically
  • Updates in real time when videos are added, removed, or reordered
  • No sign-in required

Privacy

This extension collects no data whatsoever. It reads only the video duration elements already visible on your screen and does all processing locally in your browser. No tracking, no analytics, no external requests.

Note: YouTube's UI loads approximately 200 videos at a time. Scroll down to load more videos for an accurate count on very large playlists.
